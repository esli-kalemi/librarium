Add books and resources

About us:

Our story

Many institutions struggle to manage, catalog and monitor books and resources. Librarium brings everything together in one place helping administrators organize books, manage inventory, track customer activity and monitor sales with ease.

Our mission

“Our mission is to help bookstores and libraries manage their operations efficiently through simple, modern and accessible technology.”
